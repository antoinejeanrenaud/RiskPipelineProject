Database name: risk.sqlite
Location: db/risk.sqlite

-----------------------------------------------------
Table: raw_positions
-----------------------------------------------------
Description:
    Contains the position data for three business lines (Prop, Copper, and ZincLead).
    Each row represents a futures contract position, including its maturity,
    contract type, business line, strategy, and other trade characteristics.

Columns:
    - MATURITY: Contract maturity date
    - CONTRACTTYPE: Type of contract (e.g., Future)
    - BUSINESS LINE: Name of the business line (e.g., Copper, Prop, ZincLead)
    - STRATEGY: Trading strategy (e.g., Arbitrage)
    - METAL: Underlying metal for the position
    - EXCHANGE: Exchange where the contract is traded (e.g., LME, CMX)
    - CURRENCY: Currency of the contract (e.g., USD)
    - LONGSHORT: Indicates if the position is Long (L) or Short (S)
    - VOLUME: Trade volume of the position
    - UNIT: Unit of measurement for the volume (e.g., MT, LB)
    - business_line: Added automatically by the ingestion script to identify the source file

-----------------------------------------------------
Table: raw_prices
-----------------------------------------------------
Description:
    Contains historical price data for each metal and maturity date.
    Used to calculate historical Value-at-Risk (VaR) and other risk metrics.

Columns:
    - Price Date: Date of the quoted price
    - Maturity: Contract maturity date
    - QuoteValue: Price or quote value for that contract
    - Metal: Metal name (e.g., Copper, Zinc, Lead)
    - Exchange: Exchange of the quoted contract (e.g., LME)
    - Unit: Unit of the quote (e.g., USD/MT)
